Contact Us

Have questions about volunteering with Sarel Israel or need more information about how to get involved? We’re here to help. Our team is dedicated to providing all the details you need to make a difference through our volunteer programs.

You can reach out to us through the contact form below or via our social media channels. Whether you're looking for more information on how to apply, seeking partnership opportunities, or have feedback on your experience with us, we are always happy to assist you.

Your participation and support are invaluable to us as we work together for a brighter and more peaceful future. Thank you for your interest in Sarel Israel Volunteers.
